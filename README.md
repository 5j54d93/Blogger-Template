Design Blogger's theme（template）using Bootstrap with Blogger's special syntax！This Blogger template will always show 5 latest and 10 most popular articles！This template could also show a selected feature article on top of home.  
There are social media links on top-right of navigation bar. On the sidebar, there are About、Views and Followers！You could also show license at the bottom of all pages.  
And this Blogger template could automatic switch between Day theme and Nithgt theme！
